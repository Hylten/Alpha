## Summary

The text presents a vision of a redesigned, highly technical future for Asset‑Based Lending (ABL), centered on **predictive liquidity** as the core operating principle. The argument is that traditional, human‑driven underwriting is too slow, too subjective, and too noisy for modern institutional and family‑office environments. Fund III represents a new cycle that requires precision, automation, and real‑time credit intelligence.

• Predictive liquidity is the foundation of next‑generation ABL, replacing human intuition with machine‑driven precision.  
• The **Liquidity Index Delta (LID)** becomes the primary anchor for pricing, covenants, and underwriting velocity.  
• **Algorithmic collateral intelligence** transforms assets into continuously updated statistical entities rather than static documentation.

• The “Roials‑Alpha” model emphasizes zero friction, zero lag, and zero emotional drift.
